From 20-year-old property investor to cross-border portfolio of over 10 SG & China properties

Native Chongqing, China, Zeng began her property investment journey at the young age of 20. Demonstrating an entrepreneurial spirit and an astute eye for opportunities, she built and managed a diverse portfolio exceeding 20 properties at her peak. These holdings spanned Chongqing and Shanghai, including prestigious luxury developments such as ShangHai Xintiandi and ChongQing Tiandi, properties developed by Hong Kong-listed Shui On Land.

Her early successes include selling a property to her sister at a 50% discount, which still yielded a fourfold return. Several other properties appreciated substantially, some growing tenfold. These wins bolstered her confidence and laid the foundation for her methodical, ROI-driven investment approach.

Today, Zeng owns over 10 properties across Singapore and China, with the majority situated in Singapore. Her portfolio comprises a mix of condominium units, landed homes, and commercial properties such as shops/shophouses. These assets are strategically located across key areas including city fringes, the CBD, and Chinatown. Her deep cross-border investment expertise allows her to offer clients unique perspectives that maximise both capital appreciation and rental yields.

Amy Zeng's Property Investment Strategy

Capital Appreciation & ROI-Driven Approach

Amy's investment philosophy is anchored in capital appreciation and return on investment. Her Geylang property, once difficult to finance, yielded strong results with rental income doubling over 15 years. Her sea-view apartment at International Plaza was converted to office use, generating 30–40% above market rents before being reverted to residential and rented out at $6,500/month—far above surrounding units.

"As an investor, ROI is key. We monitor rapid market changes and adapt accordingly."

She also demonstrated market foresight during COVID-19, replacing a vacated Chinatown retail tenant with a higher-paying one shortly after. Her ability to seize timing opportunities, adapt quickly, and generate strong returns consistently sets her apart.

Zeng's Investment Strategy: ROI-Driven, Diversified, and Adaptive

Zeng's business acumen shapes her investment strategy, which focuses on achieving strong ROI and portfolio diversification. She invests in retail and commercial properties, including HDB shops and strata units in CBD, Chinatown, and Geylang. Some shops were leased to KTV operators, yielding high returns. Her Geylang property, initially challenging to finance, ultimately proved highly profitable with rental rates doubling over 15 years.

Covid-19 contribution & leadership

During the peak of the Covid-19 pandemic, Zeng applied her leadership and strategic acumen beyond real estate by stepping into a national healthcare role. She used her investor mindset — focused on strategy and execution — to support Singapore's pandemic response. She was GM and shareholder of Acumen Diagnostics, a joint venture with SGX-listed Q&M Dental Group and biotech firm ARL. Acumen was awarded the Covid-19 Resilience Certificate by then-Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong.

Zeng remained tireless in her efforts until the Covid-19 outbreak was controlled and Acumen was listed under Q&M Aoxin. During this time, she also managed long-term property clients before returning full-time to real estate.
